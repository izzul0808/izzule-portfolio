About Me

Who am i?

I am Mohd Izzul Ikhwan Bin Mohd Yusof a 24-year-old graduate of Sultan Idris University of Education (UPSI) with a background in computer science for my minor and information technology for my major. At UPSI, my primary area of study is education. I made the dean's list each semester for four years in a row, and I graduated with first class honours (CGPA 3.80).

In addition, I participated in two teaching trainings, and one industrial training while attending UPSI. I finished two two-month teaching internships at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Senawang, Negeri Sembilan, and Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Lubok China, Melaka. Afterwards, I completed a two-month industrial training programme at INMA Tech Sdn. Bhd., a company that develops systems among other things.

The following sums up what I encountered during my four years—eight semesters—of study at UPSI.
